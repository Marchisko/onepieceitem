Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

Nakama ("we", "us", or "our") is a One Piece Trading Card Game collection tracker. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account information: If you create an account, we collect your email address and a hashed password.
  • Usage data: Basic app usage information (pages visited, filters used) to improve the app experience.

If you use Nakama without creating an account, your collection is stored locally on your device and we do not collect any personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To sync your collection across devices (account users only)
  • To provide and improve the Nakama service
  • To respond to support requests

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Account data is stored securely using Supabase, a third-party database provider, with servers located in the European Union. Local (non-account) data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

Nakama uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (Privacy Policy)
  • Vercel — application hosting (Privacy Policy)
  • wsrv.nl — image proxy service for card images

5. Card Images

Card images displayed in Nakama are sourced from official One Piece Card Game resources and community wikis. Nakama does not host or store card images on its own servers.

6. Children's Privacy

Nakama is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Export your collection data

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email address below.

8. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at privacy@nakama-tcg.app. We will process your request within 30 days.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
